Turn raw conversations into structured files with clear save commands.

### Who it is for
- people who lose useful context in long chats
- people who want cleaner note routing

Useful ideas often stay trapped in chats.
This tool helps classify them and save them into the right place faster.

- quickstart guide
- examples
- launcher script
- simple packaging docs

1. paste raw text into your Router chat
2. get classification and save command
3. run the command
4. keep your notes structured
